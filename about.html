About RoomSync

A roommate matching platform focused on compatibility, usability, and student-friendly housing support.

Our Mission

RoomSync was created to help students and young professionals find roommates who match their lifestyle and living habits. Instead of only focusing on rent or location, the platform encourages users to think about compatibility, communication, and comfort.

Why It Matters

Shared living environments can become stressful when people have different expectations around cleanliness, schedules, guests, pets, or study habits. RoomSync aims to reduce roommate conflict by helping users connect through shared preferences and routines.

Project Goals

This website demonstrates responsive web design, functional navigation, accessibility awareness, clean code organization, and basic interactivity using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
